What is DSA Form Generator?

DSA Form Generator is an app that allows the Head of Department to upload and store all the routines on the cloud and get the DSA form for individual teachers.

How to enter the teacher name?

Please enter the correct name for e.g. input should be 'John Wick' for teacher John Wick

Text field is not case sensitive.

Please mention the post as well for e.g. 'Dr. John Wick' or 'Prof. Dr. John Wick'

How do I download dsa form file?

First use the text field on this page and write the name of the teacher, then you will be able to download dsa form files.
